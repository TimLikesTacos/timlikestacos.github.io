About Tim

Tim Reed is a current resident of Portland OR.  He grew up in the Seattle, WA area, went to the University of Washington where he graduated with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering.

From there, he served in the US Navy aboard submarines.  After about seven years of service, Tim left the Navy and workied for the Tennessee Valley Authority as a Senior Reactor Operator and later as a Shift Operations Manager.

Supporting his wife's career, he left Tennessee and moved to Puerto Rico for 3 years.  Here he decided to adjust his career path and started attending Old Dominion University, working towards a B.S. in Comuter Science.  He currently has a 4.0 GPA and will graduate in May 2021.

Software Projects

Rust Sudoku Library

A Rust library that not only solves Sudoku puzzles, but does so using human style solving strategies such as "Hidden Tuples", "Pointing Candidates", and "X-Wings".  The library keeps track of the solving techniques, which allows for demonstrating a solving flowpath, along with a difficulty rating based on the complexity of the solving techniques needed.

Peronsal Name Extractor

A Java project for a college course.  Using maching learning, the Personal Name Extractor finds names within text and surrounds the name with identification tags.  It uses the CLI, or text file inputs.
